Your peace of mind is our priority

1. Booking Policy

All bookings must be made through our official channels, either online, by phone, or in person. A deposit may be required to secure reservations. Final payments should be made at least 14 days before the travel date.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be submitted in writing. Fees may apply based on the proximity of the travel date. Cancellations made within 7 days of departure may not be eligible for a refund.

3. Refund Policy

Refunds depend on the service provider's terms (airlines, hotels, etc.). Processing may take 7–14 business days. Non-refundable services are clearly indicated during booking.

4. Travel Documentation

It is the traveler’s responsibility to have valid passports, visas, and any required health certificates. We provide support but cannot guarantee visa approval or entry permission.
